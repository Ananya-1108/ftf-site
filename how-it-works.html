02
Smart Shopping

Reverse Lookup

Know what you want to make but not sure what to buy? Enter any dish name and get a complete, organized shopping list — with brand recommendations and what to avoid.

🏷️
Brand recommendations

For every canned or packaged item, we name the best brand and explain why (e.g. lowest sodium, no preservatives).

📦
Bulk buying tips

Know which items save you more per ounce when bought in bulk at Costco vs. your campus store.

03
Nutrition Focused

Health Recipes

Got lab results from a doctor visit? Or just know you're low on iron, vitamin D, or protein? Enter your concern and get 10 recipes specifically designed to help.

🔬
Science-backed explanations

Every recipe explains exactly how it helps — e.g. "The spinach provides 3.6mg of iron. Vitamin C in the lemon boosts absorption by 300%."

⚠️
Blocker foods listed

You'll learn what foods to avoid pairing together — like calcium blocking iron absorption.

🩺
Medical disclaimer included

We always remind you to consult your doctor. This is food guidance, not medical advice.
